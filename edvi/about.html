ABOUT US

This project is geared toward empowering youths in STEAM-related fields. This will encourage the students to pursue future careers in Engineering and STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts/Agric and Mathematics) related fields. Our flagship projects will provide youths with a platform to share their aspirations and ideas with the world. Our STEAM initiatives emphasizes how technology will be used to solve the problems in Africa, using integrated inquiry-based learning heavily based on hands-on learning experience. We give youths the platform chance to explore STEAM disciplines while being introduced to technologies that use locally available resources to solve significant problems in their local communities. We strongly believe that youths can be at the forefront through Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Maths towards achieving the UN Sustainable Development Goals through our impactful programmes.
These include;
1. SHE_ENGINEERS: A platform designed to propagate and maximize the inclusion of girls and young women in the engineering field with a strong alignment to UN SDGs; to encourage them to create a better and more sustainable future in the world.
2. HE_ENGINEERS: A platform tailored to propagate and maximize the inclusion of boys and young men in the engineering field with a strong alignment to UN SDGs to encourage them to create a better and more sustainable future in the world.
And other upcoming STEAM_CAREER PROGRAMMES
