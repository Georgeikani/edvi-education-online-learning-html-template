IMPLEMENTATION STRATEGY

Through boot camps, workshops, training, drills and tour visit;
Our team will be hosting the following series of career advocacy programmes.

SCOPE AND BENEFICIARIES

The project will cut across youths of different backgrounds in Nigeria. Participants are between
the ages of 14 yrs – 22 yrs. The participants are expected to be innovative thinkers, enthusiastic about
leading change, and positively unlocking opportunities in society.

  • Must have an interest in analytical and numerical analysis.
  • Students in STEAM subjects
  • Must be no more than twenty-two (22) years old.
  • A positive and growth mindset.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

    • HOW CAN YOU BENEFITS?

    1. Connect: Network with fellow stakeholders in Nigeria–Inventors, trailblazers,
    captains of industries, government officials, and mentors.
    2. Recruit: Meet your trainees and get new talent interested in your programs.
    3. Market: Promote organization to participants, guests, and other businesses in Nigeria.
    4. Give back: Mentor or volunteer during the event and support the next generation of STEAM Champions
    5. Help the helpers: Play a role in the development of young girls and boys who
    seek career opportunities in the STEAM field.
